Your context
  • Age 30, ~115 lbs, very fast metabolism
  • Goal: bulk up, get stronger, build muscle mass
  • Target areas: hips, thighs, glutes, overall strength
What doesn't work
  • Plain milk, peanut butter, cottage cheese
  • Apple juice (protein didn't break down)
  • Hot chocolate (unreliable, max 1-2 scoops)
  • Protein snow system (too inconsistent)

Protein is needed every day — not just workout days. Muscles repair and grow during rest days too.

Protein Delivery System
Primary Method
Blended Shakes (Nutribullet)
Most reliable and enjoyable method. Naked Mass unflavored blends well with frozen fruit, oat milk, yogurt. Pre-batch into 4oz glass jars on Sundays for the week. 4-day consumption window.
Secondary: Greek Yogurt
Stir in 1-1.5 scoops. Handles mixing well. Use when blending isn't practical.
Secondary: Strawberry Lemonade
Shake well. Works in a pinch but not a go-to option.

Nutribullet Loading Order

  1. Banana (first, at the bottom)
  2. Dry / flavor ingredients (cookie butter, spices)
  3. Protein powder
  4. Liquid last — so liquid hits the blade first
Smoothie Lab

One variable changed per batch. Sweet over tart. Silky/creamy textures. Pre-batch Sundays. Workout days = 2 bottles, rest days = 1.
