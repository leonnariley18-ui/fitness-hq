Fitness Ecosystem Master Plan

The north star document — why this exists and where it's going
v1.3 · April 25, 2026 · Active
The Philosophy

This system started in February 2026 as a yoga-centered practice. It evolved — as all good things do — into something more intentional. The ecosystem is now calisthenics-first, built around two parallel strength progressions (push-ups and pistol squats) with yoga as a need-based practice, flexibility work woven throughout, and nutrition supporting it all.

The goal was never perfection or rigid scheduling. It was about showing up consistently, building something real over time, and reclaiming a version of yourself that felt strong, bendy, and capable. That's still the goal. Everything in this ecosystem exists to support that.

What's Active
  • 01
    Push-Up Progression ↗ Upper Body
    8-week structured progression toward full floor push-ups. Four 2-week blocks, 3x per week. Currently Block 2.
  • 02
    Pistol Squat Progression ↗ Lower Body
    4-block progression toward a full pistol squat. Runs on push-up rest days so upper and lower body always alternate. Starting May 1, 2026.
  • 03
    Yoga Strap Flexibility Routine ↗ Flexibility
    12-14 min routine focused on hamstrings, hips, and lower body. 3-4x per week, always last in any session. Long-term goal: a split. Ms. Bendy is a title to be reclaimed.
  • 04
    Targeted Practice ↗ Flexibility
    Three versions (V1, V2, V3) generated by CC based on struggle poses from Google Calendar. Loaded into App #1. No cool-down needed.
  • 05
    App #1 — Yoga Flow Generator ↗ Yoga
    AI yoga flow generation for need-based practice. Two modes: Flow Practice and Targeted Practice. Opens in Claude.
  • 06
    CC — Yoga Coaching Claude ↗ Yoga
    Dedicated Claude project. Reads Google Calendar directly, identifies struggle poses, generates targeted practice JSONs, and creates calendar events. Used as needed.
  • 07
    Smoothie Lab ↗ Nutrition
    Blended shakes as primary protein delivery. Naked Mass unflavored. Biscoff Baby weekly batch. Nutribullet. Iterative recipe development — one variable per batch.
  • 08
    Warm-Up System ↗ Foundation
    Three tiers: SFB (5 min), Quick + LEG (3 min), Quick - LEG (~90 sec). Every session starts with a warm-up. No exceptions.
  • 09
    Workout Logger ↗ App
    Log every session — anchors, warm-up, duration, vibe, notes. Output pastes into calendar event descriptions.
  • 10
    Check-In App ↗ App
    Bi-weekly check-in form. Fill in answers, generate formatted prompt, paste into Claude fitness project chat.
  • —
    AM Stretch Routine 🔒 Locked
    5-10 min morning flexibility routine targeting hips, hamstrings, and split goal. Unlocks after completing Pistol Squat Block 1.
  • —
    AM Stretch Routine Locked
    1-2 morning stretches, 5-10 min, no warm-up needed. Targeting flexibility, hips, hamstrings, and split goal. Unlocks when Pistol Squat Block 1 is complete (~May 14, 2026).
  • —
    Yoga App Ecosystem (Apps #2/3, #4) ↗ Shelved
    Analytics and flow editing apps. Shelved April 2026 — CC and App #1 free-text cover the same needs more flexibly. Preserved for reference.

Rules That Never Change
Rules That Never Change ▼
  • Warm-up always first — no exceptions
  • Quick - LEG is for push-up only days exclusively — never before pistol squats
  • Post-push-up stretches always immediately after push-ups
  • Post-pistol squat stretches always immediately after pistol squats
  • Strap routine always last in any session
  • Full rest is always valid
  • Log every session in the Workout Logger
