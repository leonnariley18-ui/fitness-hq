How It Works

Targeted Practice — Key Notes

  • Generated by CC (Yoga Coaching Claude) based on your struggle poses from Google Calendar
  • Load the downloaded JSON into App #1 ↗ via Targeted Practice mode
  • App #1 handles voice guidance, alignment cues, hold timers, and side switching automatically
  • New versions generated by CC as your practice evolves and new struggle poses emerge
  • Each version has a duplicatable calendar event — duplicate it every time you do the practice
