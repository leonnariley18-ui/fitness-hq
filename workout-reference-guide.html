Warm-Up Quick Reference
SFB
5 minutes
Anything with legs, yoga, or targeted practice
Quick + LEG
3 minutes
Rushed sessions involving legs
Quick - LEG
~90 seconds
Push-ups only. Never before pistol squats.
The Anchors — Pick One or More in This Order
01
Push-Ups ↗
~25 min
Quick - LEG then push-ups then post-push-up stretches
02
Pistol Squats ↗
~25 min
SFB or Quick + LEG then pistol squat session then post-pistol squat stretches
03
Yoga — App #1 ↗
~20-70 min
SFB or Quick + LEG then yoga flow via App #1. Opens in a new tab.
04
Targeted Practice ↗
~15-22 min
SFB or Quick + LEG then targeted practice via App #1. No cool-down needed.
05
Strap Routine ↗
~20 min
SFB or Quick + LEG then strap routine. Always last in any session.
The Layering Rule

Stack anything you want — just keep the order above.

  • Post-push-up stretches always right after push-ups
  • Post-pistol squat stretches always right after pistol squats
  • Strap routine always last
  • Rest is always valid
  • Log your session in the Workout Logger after
