Fitness Ecosystem Master Plan

The North Star Document
v1.2  ·  April 13, 2026  ·  Active
Why This Document Exists

The fitness ecosystem has evolved significantly since it started in February 2026. What began as a yoga-centered system has shifted into a calisthenics-first approach with yoga as a need-based practice. This document is the single source of truth for the big picture. The detailed "how" lives in the referenced documents — this one answers "what" and "why."

Active Components
01 — UPPER BODY
Push-Up Progression
8-week structured progression toward full floor push-ups. Four 2-week blocks. Currently Block 2. Runs 3x per week.
→ Calisthenics Master Plan
02 — LOWER BODY
Pistol Squat Progression
4-block progression toward a full pistol squat. Runs on push-up rest days — upper and lower alternate. Starting April 2026.
→ Calisthenics Master Plan
03 — FLEXIBILITY
Yoga Strap Routine
12-14 min routine focused on hamstrings, hips, lower body. 3-4x per week, always last in any session. Goal: a split. Ms. Bendy.
→ Yoga Strap Flexibility Routine doc
04 — YOGA
App #1 — Yoga Flow Generator
AI yoga flow generation for need-based practice. Two modes: Flow Practice (generate + free-text customization) and Targeted Practice (load CC-generated JSON).
→ jkhgfvdcgvhbjnnn_html.html
05 — COACHING
CC — Yoga Coaching Claude
Dedicated Claude project. Reads Google Calendar, identifies struggle poses, generates targeted practice JSON + creates calendar event. Used as needed.
→ Separate Claude project
06 — NUTRITION
Smoothie Lab
Blended shakes as primary protein delivery. Naked Mass unflavored. Biscoff Baby weekly batch. Nutribullet. Flexible for hectic life periods.
→ Fitness & Nutrition Systems doc
07 — FOUNDATION
Warm-Up System
Three options: SFB (5 min) before anything with legs, yoga, or targeted practice  ·  Quick + LEG (3 min) for rushed sessions  ·  Quick - LEG (~90 sec) for push-ups only — never before pistol squats.
→ Warm-Up Routines doc
08 — TRACKING
Workout Logger App
HTML app for logging each session. Select anchors, warm-up, duration (timer or manual), vibe, and notes. Generates a formatted log to paste into calendar event descriptions. Replaces manual description editing.
→ workout-logger.html
09 — CHECK-INS
Check-In App
HTML app for bi-weekly check-ins. Fill in answers, set date range, hit generate — produces a formatted prompt ready to paste into the Claude fitness project chat. Replaces Google Forms and CSV workflow entirely.
→ check-in-app.html
Shelved — April 2026
Yoga App Ecosystem (Apps #2/3 and #4)
Originally planned analytics, targeted drill, and flow editing apps. Shelved — yoga is need-based, insufficient data for app-based analysis. CC + App #1 free-text cover the same needs more flexibly. Preserved in full for reference.
Long-Term Goals
💪
Pull-Ups
Natural next step after push-up Block 4. To be formalized as a progression when the time is right.
🦵🏿
Pistol Squat
Endpoint of lower body progression (Block 4). Target: August 2026.
🦂
A Split (Ms. Bendy)
Long-term flexibility goal. Built via strap routine and yoga. Timeline: 6-12 months of consistent work.
Rules That Never Change
  • Warm-up always first — no exceptions
  • Quick - LEG is for push-up only days exclusively — never before pistol squats
  • Post-push-up stretches always immediately after push-ups
  • Post-pistol squat stretches always immediately after pistol squats
  • Strap routine always last in any session
  • Full rest is always valid
