Yoga App Ecosystem Master Plan

Master Plan & Specifications
v5.1  ·  SHELVED April 10, 2026  ·  Preserved for reference
Shelved — April 2026

This plan has been shelved.

As of the April 2026 ecosystem redesign, yoga has shifted to a need-based practice rather than the center of the fitness ecosystem. Apps #2/3 and #4 will not be built at this time — there isn't enough consistent yoga data to make analytics-based apps useful.

App #1 remains fully active and in use. The yoga coaching need is now handled by CC (Yoga Coaching Claude), a dedicated Claude project. App #1's free-text field addition covers what App #4 would have done.

This document is preserved in full in case yoga becomes a larger focus again in the future.

→ See Fitness Ecosystem Master Plan v1.1 for the current north star document.
App Status
App #1
Yoga Flow Generator ↗
✅ Active
AI-powered yoga flow generation. Two modes: Flow Practice (generate + free-text customization before generation and on regeneration) and Targeted Practice (load CC-generated JSON with pose education, voice guidance, alignment cues, hold timers).
Free-text fields added April 2026 · Targeted Practice mode added April 2026
Apps #2 / #3
Yoga Pattern Analyzer & Targeted Practice Builder
🚫 Shelved
Originally planned to analyze Google Calendar ICS data, identify struggle pose patterns, and generate targeted drill sessions. Replaced by CC (Yoga Coaching Claude project) which does this conversationally and more flexibly.
App #4
Flow Editor
🚫 Shelved
Originally planned to allow editing of App #1 flows (swap poses, adjust duration, remove things). Replaced by App #1's new free-text field on regeneration, which covers this conversationally.
