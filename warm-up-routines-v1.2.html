Standard Full-Body Warm-Up
▼
Use before yoga, strap routine, pistol squats, or any full session. Keep moving the entire 5 minutes. You should feel warm and energized by the end.
  • 01
    Neck Rolls
    Gently roll head in half-circles, side to side. Slow and controlled.
  • 02
    Chest Opener
    Arms out to sides, bring forward (like clapping), then back out wide.
  • 03
    Hip Circles
    Feet planted, circle hips both directions.
  • 04
    Shoulder Rolls with Arm Swings
    Swing arms forward then backward, bending knees each time arms come down.
  • 05
    Cat-Cow (on the ground)
    Inhale: drop belly, lift chest (cow). Exhale: round spine (cat). Move with breath.
  • 06
    Wide Stance Toe Reach
    Wide stance, reach toward toes. Hamstrings and inner thighs.
  • 07
    Side Lunges
    Step wide to one side, bend that knee, other leg straight. Alternate sides.
  • 08
    High Knees with Arm Pulls
    Bring knee to chest while pulling arms down. Alternating legs.
  • 09
    Standing Toe Reach Kicks
    Back straight, kick leg up toward hands. Hamstrings and balance.
  • 10
    Cross-Body Step Kicks
    Kick leg across and back while moving arms to stretch chest. Keep elbows up.
  • 11
    Hip Circles (Standing Fire Hydrant)
    Lift one leg off ground, circle the hip. Both directions, both legs.
  • 12
    Ankle Circles (Pointed Toe)
    Point toe away, hold 2-3 sec. Flex back toward you, hold 2-3 sec. Both ankles.
  • 13
    Side Stretch
    Reach one arm up and over to the side. Both sides.
  • 14
    Wrist Circles
    Arms extended, make fists, circle wrists both directions. Preps wrists for floor work.
Quick Versions

Quick + LEG (3 min): Any rushed session involving legs. Keep all exercises including the ones marked LEG

Quick - LEG (~90 sec): Push-up only days. Skip ALL LEG exercises. Never use before pistol squats.

5-6 reps each · LEG = skip on push-up only days
Quick Full-Body Warm-Up
▼
  • 01
    Neck Rolls
    Quick half-circles, both directions.
  • 02
    Arm Circles
    Big circles forward then back, 10 each direction.
  • 03
    Hip Circles LEG
    Both directions, 10 circles each way.
  • 04
    Cat-Cow (on the ground)
    8-10 reps, sync with breath.
  • 05
    Wide Stance Toe Reach LEG
    6 reps, reach down and up.
  • 06
    High Knees LEG
    Quick high knees, 20 total (10 each leg).
  • 07
    Cross-Body Step Kicks LEG
    Dynamic movement, 10 total.
  • 08
    Ankle Circles and Wrist Circles
    Quick point-and-flex each ankle, then wrist circles both directions.
  • 09
    Side Stretch
    Reach up and over, both sides, hold 3 seconds each.
Post-Push-Up Stretches

Post-Push-Up Stretches

4 minutes · after every push-up session · non-negotiable
  • 01
    Chest Opener (Doorway Stretch) — 60 sec · Stand in doorway, arms on frame at shoulder height, step forward. OR clasp hands behind back and lift.
  • 02
    Cross-Body Shoulder Stretch — 30 sec each side · One arm across body at chest level, other arm gently pulls at elbow.
  • 03
    Tricep Stretch — 30 sec each side · One arm overhead, bend elbow so hand reaches down your back, other hand gently pushes elbow back.
  • 04
    Child's Pose — 60 sec · Kneel, sit hips back toward heels, extend arms forward, forehead to floor. Breathe and relax.
  • 05
    Wrist Stretch — 30 sec each direction · Hands on floor, fingers pointing toward body, lean weight back. Repeat with fingers pointing away.
Post-Pistol Squat Stretches

Post-Pistol Squat Stretches

4 minutes · after every pistol squat session · non-negotiable
  • 01
    Standing Quad Stretch — 30 sec each leg · Bend one knee, bring foot toward glute, hold ankle. Hold wall for balance if needed.
  • 02
    Low Lunge Hip Flexor Stretch — 45 sec each side · Step one foot forward into low lunge, back knee on floor. Sink hips forward and down.
  • 03
    Standing Figure Four — 30 sec each side · Cross one ankle over opposite knee, sit back like lowering into a chair.
  • 04
    Seated Forward Fold — 60 sec · Legs extended, loop strap around feet if needed. Hinge from hips with long spine. Full posterior chain.
  • 05
    Supine Figure Four — 45 sec each side · Lie on back. Cross right ankle over left thigh, flex foot. Draw both legs gently toward chest.
